Crystal Collector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the computers' score. Should you go over, you lose.

The value of each crystal is random (each game), and hidden.

Figure out the values before it's too late! Good luck!
